About IMGFY

IMGFY is a web-based image processing and image forgery detection system designed for academic and practical use. Our platform helps users detect copy-move forgery in digital images using advanced computer vision and machine learning techniques.

What we offer:

  • Multiple image processing like removing noise, applying filters etc.
  • Multiple detection algorithms: SIFT, SURF, ORB, DCT, CNN, etc.
  • User-friendly dashboard to manage uploads and results
  • Analytics and performance tracking of algorithms
  • Secure login system with user-specific history

Our goal is to assist students, researchers, and professionals in identifying digital forgeries efficiently.
