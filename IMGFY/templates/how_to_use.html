How to Use the Copy-Move Forgery Detection System

  1. Register or Login: Create an account or log in to access features.
  2. Dashboard: View statistics of previously uploaded images and detection results.
  3. Upload: Go to the Upload page to select an image and choose a forgery detection algorithm.
  4. Algorithm Options: Choose from methods like SIFT, ORB, BRISK, LBP, CNN, etc.
  5. View Results: After upload, you'll see the original image and forgery detection output with highlighted forged regions.
  6. Download: Save the processed image and result report to your system.
  7. History: View a history of your uploaded images and their results.
  8. Logout: Use the logout button to securely exit your session.

If you encounter any issues, feel free to contact us via the contact form or send your feedback.
